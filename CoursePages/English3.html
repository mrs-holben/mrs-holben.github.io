English 3

Focus of Course:

English III concentrates on reading great literature through our biblical worldview lens. We will work on understanding Aristotle’s “Truth and Beauty” principals in composition and literature while learning that great literature reflects that we are all made of dust and the breath of God. We will see how the lives of characters are caught between sin and redemption. This course meets the requirements for one English credit.
